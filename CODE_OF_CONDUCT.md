# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insults, or personal/political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report abuse to **info@jorahone.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
